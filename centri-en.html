Centres and Institutions

/DH.arc

The Centre for Digital Humanities Advanced Research (DH.arc) is part of the Department of Classical Philology and Italian Studies (FICLIT) at the University of Bologna. The centre brings together students, researchers, IT staff and faculty from FICLIT, the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (DISI), and affiliated institutions.

Centro di ricerca internazionale sugli Archivi digitali di persona

The International Research Centre on Personal Digital Archives is an initiative of the University of Bologna that focuses on the issue of long-term archiving and preservation of personal digital archives, as well as personal digital memories in general. These memories include documents, photographs, audio and video recordings, and other digital content produced by individuals or research activities. The Centre reviews the scientific literature in this field, including international research projects, and promotes knowledge and best practices in digital archiving. Additionally, the Centre has collaborated with the Summer School in Web and Social Media Archiving and Preservation, offering training and knowledge exchange opportunities for those interested in this area.

Clarin-IT - Infrastruttura Comune Italiana per le Risorse e le Tecnologie Linguistiche

CLARIN-IT is the Italian node of CLARIN (Common Language Resources and Technology Infrastructure), a research infrastructure that integrates language resources and advanced tools for the exploration, access, and analysis of digital content at the European level. This goal is achieved through a network of national centres across Europe that provide repositories of digital linguistic data, advanced tools, and cutting-edge physical and digital archives. Thanks to a unified access service, these resources are available to the research community to conduct high-level investigations, fostering innovation and competitiveness.
